About This Project

Sales Analytics Dashboard

A comprehensive view of 20,000 unified sales transactions sourced from ventes_unified_20k.csv. This dashboard helps stakeholders explore revenue streams, identify top-performing regions and product categories, monitor stock movements, and understand customer segments -- all from a single, interactive page.

Purpose

Transform raw CSV data into actionable business insights without any backend.

Technology

Pure HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. No frameworks, no server-side processing.

Privacy

All data stays in your browser. Nothing is uploaded or tracked externally.
